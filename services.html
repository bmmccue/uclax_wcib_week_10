How It Works

Choosing Your Project

We transform 1, 2, or 3-car garage spaces and sheds into small studios or closets/storage. We can design fully functional studios, complete with bathrooms and kitchens.

Inspecting the Space

We’ll come to your home and assess your garage or shed space. Once we clarify your needs, we’ll offer suggestions and develop a solution to design your new space.

Construction & Design

Once you’ve approved our proposal, we’ll get to work! We typically complete projects in 2-3 weeks.
