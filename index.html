From Garage to Studio

Welcome to Instant Studios!

Our garage conversions are a quick and practical solution to a lack of space in your home. Whether you’re looking to accommodate a loved one while ensuring their independence and privacy or trying to earn some extra income, our team will transform your garage or shed into a fully functional studio or closet space!

Why Convert Your Garage?

More Space On a Budget

Converting your garage is a simple way to add more space to your home without breaking the bank, and much more affordable than buying a prefabricated space.

Keeping Loved Ones Close

Adding extra studio space can help keep elderly relatives close or accommodate just returned college aged children, while helping them maintain their privacy and independence.

Earning Extra Income

An extra studio or converted shed can be rented to long or short term residents.
